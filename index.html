About Us

Accelerate Field Hockey is a grassroots initiative dedicated to building and inspiring the next generation of field hockey players in Hawke's Bay. Our goal is to create more opportunities for youth engagement, coaching development, and community support in the sport we love.

About Me

My name is Corby, and I have played for the Hawkes Bay U18s, Christchurch Division One, Rankin Cup, and Australian Nationals. I am passionate about hockey and accelerating the growth of young players here in the Hawkes Bay!
